conductor

Jack Capstaff believes that music can inspire people and enrich lives, both for musicians and audiences.   With his energetic approach and commitment to the diversification of westerern classical music, Jack has brought music to new audiences and worked to make the arts accessible to many in underserved communities.

composer

"A prolific composer and arranger, Jack continues to push the boundaries of possibility in orchestral and band mediums, bringing new and exciting music, projects, and formats to audiences across the country."        

communicator

As a public speaker, and advocate for the arts; Jack has worked with organisations such as Brass Bands England, the Brass Band Conductor's Association, and given talks and presentations both at festivals, conferences, and the Royal Philharmonic Society Awards.
